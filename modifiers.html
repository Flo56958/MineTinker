MineTinker-Modifiers

The Information given on this page is only representing the default modifier configuration.
Servers running MineTinker can alter the maximum Level, crafting recipes, name or can disable the modifier completly. 
Each MineTinker-item has a limited number of Modifier-Slots. If the item has no free slots available, you can not add more modifiers on to it. Each level of a Modifier will require an extra free slot.

Interaction with Enchantments: Some modifiers apply vanilla enchantments on to the tool when applied. This will overwrite the current enchantment level.
Modifiers are applied through the anvil.

Aquaphilic

Enhance your Armor for underwater use. Applies Depth Strider, Aqua Affinity and Respiration on the specific armor piece.

Only for Boots and Helmets

Max. Level: 3 

Auto-Smelt

Chance to smelt the ore while mining. Does not work under water and will smelt coal into smoke. (20% Chance per Level)

Only for Axes, Pickaxes and Shovels

Max. Level: 5

Beheading

Chance to get the mobs head dropped (works also with player heads) when killing the mob. (10% Chance per Level)

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with a Wither-Skeleton-Skull. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

Only for Axes, Bows, Swords and Tridents

Max. Level: 10

Directing

Mob-Loot goes directly into the players inventory.

Only for Axes, Bows, Swords and Tridents

Max. Level: 1

Ender

Teleport yourself using a bow or a trident to the location the projectile landed. If you hit an entity you swap positions.

Only for Bows and Tridents

Max. Level: 2

Experienced

Chance to drop additional XP when using the item. (2% chance per Level)

For every MineTinker-Tool, -Weapon and -Armor

Max. Level: 10

Extra-Modifier

Increase the available slots of the MineTinker-item

For every MineTinker-Tool, -Weapon and -Armor

Max. Level: Unlimited 

Fiery

Enflame your enemies! Applies Fire Aspect or Flame on the weapon.

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with a blaze-rod. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

Only for Axes, Bows and Swords

Max. Level: 2

Freezing

Freeze the water around you. Applies Frost walker.

Only for Boots

Max. Level: 3 

Glowing

Give yourself a competitiv advantage with this modifier. It applies a glowing effect on the damaged entity. Each Level increases the duration of the effect.

Only for Axes, Bows, Swords and Tridents

Max. Level: 3

Haste

Enhance your Tools for faster dig speed. Applies Efficiency on the Tool.

Only for Axes, Pickaxes and Shovels

Max. Level: 5

Infinity

Don't worry about how many arrows you have or your trident lands. Applies Infinity or Loyalty on the Weapon.

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with an arrow. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

Only for Bows and Tridents

Max. Level: 1

Knockback

Knock your enemies back to where they came from. Applies Knockback or Punch on the Weapon.

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with a block of TNT. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

Only for Axe, Bows, Swords and Tridents

Max. Level: 5

Life-Steal

Chance to damage your Enemies and heal yourself at their cost.

Only for Axes, Bows, Swords and Tridents

Max. Level: 3

Light-Weight

You do not fall as hard. Applies Feather Falling to the armor piece.

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with a feather. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

Only for Boots

Max. Level: 3

Luck

Enhance your Items with more loot drop. Applies Fortune or Looting on the MineTinker-Item.

Only for Axes, Bows, Hoes, Pickaxes, Shovels, Swords and Tridents 

Max. Level: 5

Melting

Make more Damage when the entity is on fire or reduce the damge you take while on fire.

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with a magma block. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

Only for Axes, Bows, Chestplates, Leggings and Swords

Max. Level: 3

Poisonous

Poison your Enemies with you attacks. Applies a poison effect on the hit entity.

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with a rotten flesh. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

Only for Axes, Boots, Bows, Chestplates, Helmets, Leggings Swords and Tridents

Max. Level: 5

Power

Enhance your Tools to break more blocks, tilt more soil or harvest more crops per swing 

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with an emerald. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

Only for Axes, Hoes, Pickaxes and Shovels

Max. Level: 3

Propelling

Propell yourself into the air. Applies Riptide on the Trident like the firework-boost on the elytra when pressing shift.

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with a fireworkstar. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

Only for Elytras and Tridents
Max. Level: 3

Protecting

Enhance your Armor with more damage resistence. Applies Protection on the item.

Only for Boots, Chestplates, Elytras,

Max. Level: 5

Reinforced

Make your tools not break so easily. Applies Unbreaking on the item.

For every MineTinker-Tool, -Weapon and -Armor

Max. Level: 5

Self-Repair

Chance to repair the MineTinker-Item when using it. (10% Chance per Level)

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with a mossy cobblestone. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

For every MineTinker-Tool, -Weapon and -Armor

Max. Level: 10

Sharpness

Enhance your Weapons with more damage output. Applies Sharpness or Power.

Only for Axes, Bows, Swords and Tridents

Max. Level: 5

Shulking

Make your enemies fly. The higher the Level the higher they go.

Only for Axes, Boots, Bows, Chestplates, Elytra, Helmets, Leggings, Swords and Tridents

Max. Level: 10

Silk-Touch

Drop the mined block exactly. Applies Silk-Touch on the Tool.

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with a cobweb. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

Only for Axes, Hoes, Pickaxes and Shovels

Max. Level: 1

Soulbound

Do not drop your MineTinker-Item when dying.

For every MineTinker-Tool, -Weapon and -Armor

Max. Level: 1

Sweeping

Make more damage over a greater Area. Applies Sweeping Edge to the Weapon.

To get the Modifier you need to right-click a Bookshelf with a iron ingot. This will require 10 Levels of XP. 

Only for Swords

Max. Level: 5

Timber

Chop down trees in an instant.

Only for Axes

Max. Level: 1

Webbed

Slow down your enemies. Applies a slowness effect to your damager or the damaged entity. Higher Level will increase the effect and the duration.

Only for Axes, Boots, Bows, Chestplates, Elytras, Helmets, Leggings, Swords and Tridents

Max. Level: 3
