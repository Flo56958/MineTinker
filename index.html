MineTinker

It is a spiritual port of the well-known Forge mod Tinker's Construct. It is a server-plugin for Minecraft Spigot-Servers. MineTinker is not a 100%-port but more like a step in a simillar direction. 
30+ Modifiers, really easy to configure allmost everything.

When tools, weapons and armor are crafted in the Crafting Table, they will have special features added to them. The lore shows you which modifiers your item has and how many free Slots you have remaining.
For each block broken, for each enemy you hit, for each damage you take your Tool, Weapon or Armor piece gets Exp. When the item has aquired enough Exp it levels up, gets a free modifier slot and sometimes drops special loot.
MineTinker-Tools cannot break. Instead they become unusable when their durability gets to close to 0. Keep in mind: Armor pieces can still break. Even from MineTinker.
You can drag and drop material on the MineTinker-Item you want to repair to replenish its durability. (e.g. diamond for diamond tools)
